Introduction

First Roll

First Roll is a digital magazine designed for new photographers—whether you're loading your first roll of film or inserting your first memory card. From mastering camera settings to picking your first lens, we provide clear, beginner-friendly guidance to help you build confidence behind the camera. With practical tips, gear advice, and creative inspiration, First Roll is your starting point for capturing the world—one photo at a time.
